ECE DEPT PROFILE

Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering having academic autonomy of having its own syllabus and curriculum.

The Department of ECE is accredited by NBA under Tier -I in 2015, New Delhi, and NAAC Bengaluru.

Department started with intake of 40 studentsin 1998 and 60 students in 2016.In 2008, the department introduced a PG Program in “VLSI Design” with an intake of 18 students. Also other/new PG programs namely Digital Electronics and Communication Systems and Embedded Systems were started in the year 2010.VLSI Design (shift-II) was started in the year 2013.

Electronics graduates find jobs in the space programs, Satellites etc.
