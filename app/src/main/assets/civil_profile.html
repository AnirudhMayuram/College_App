CIVIL DEPT PROFILE

The Civil Engineering Department administers a civil engineering program that will produce graduates who are ready to work, high in quality, skilled in information technology and are professional in nature.

To become a study Program that is accepted as a model in the field of civil engineering education and leads to the application of civil engineering to provide quality professional and community services

Objectives

  • To provide students with the fundamental technical knowledge
  • To provide students with the necessary skills and practical experiences to fulfill their professional duties
  • To mould students to become future engineers,scientists,researchers and innovators
  • To make the students put constant efforts to impove the living quality of all walks of life by solving wide range of problems
