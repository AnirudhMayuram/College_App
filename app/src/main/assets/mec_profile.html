MEC DEPT PROFILE

The Department of Mechanical Engineering has come into existence in the year 1998, since inception of the college twelve batches have passed out.

The Department has 64 dedicated staff members (Teaching 54, Non Teaching 8, and supporting Staff 2), 8 staff members with PhD, 6 staff members are pursuing PhD’s, and the rest of faculty members are with PG Qualifications.

Dr. C.Vijaya Bhaskar Reddy, Head of the Department has published more than 15 papers at various National and International conferences

Prof K.L. Narayana is an author of 23 Text Books and published more than 44 research papers. Prof. O.V.K Chetty, received two awards at national level for his best research papers. He published more than 155 research papers. Prof. U. Harinath published more than 17 research papers.

The department received ISO 9001 – 2000 certificate and accredited by National Board of- Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi for its excellence in all respects.
