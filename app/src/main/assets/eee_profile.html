EEE DEPT PROFILE

The Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering was established in the year 1998 with an intake of 40 students and subsequently enhanced to 60 in the year 2001, 90 in 2009 and 120 in 2010 for B.Tech programme.

The department has been recognised as research centre by JNTUA, Ananthapuramu in 2014.

Major and Minor research projects are in the pipe line to be submitted to UGC in the current academic year 2016-17 for getting financial assistance.

The students are placed in various organizations like INFOSYS, IGATE, LEGEND, WIPRO, INFOTECH, SATYAM, SIMENS, Robert Bosch and CARITOR, APTRANSCO and APGENCO power sectors, Siridi Sai Electricals Ltd, Syntel, Toshiba Transmission and Distribution System Ltd and Cognizant Technologies Solution (CTS).

Vision

  • To produce dynamic, competent, knowledgeable electrical engineers who shall lead a Nation to a better future by establishing the strong teaching and research environment.

Mission

  • To offer quality education in Electrical and Electronics Engineering and prepare the students for professional career and higher studies.
  • To promote excellence in teaching, research, consultancy activities and positive contribution to the society
