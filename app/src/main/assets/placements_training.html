Objectives

  • The Placement Office initiates and nurtures contacts with leading organizations for campus visits and is functional throughout the year and also assists the students for Summer/Winter Industrial training it makes sure best arrangement and hospitality for officials of the visiting companies.
  • A team of Student Placement Coordinators duly assist the T&P Office also provides counseling and guidance to the students regarding the visiting organizations, selection procedure, preparation methods & resources thereby ensuring that the students are adequately equipped to face the recruitment tests.
  • The central computer facilities are adequate for an online test for about 600 students at a time and Presentations, Written Test, Group Discussion and Interviews.

Placements Consortium

As part of our Constant OUTREACH Programme, SVCET has formed a Placement Consortium to help the engineering and science students of neighbouring colleges. Students from these colleges in the nearby towns benefit immensely from this Consortium. Reputed companieshave conducted campus interviews and recruited students through the Consortium.
