TRANSPORT

Transport Division is headed by a Head of Transport Division is formed to take care of the smooth operation of buses

Duties

Allocating bus routes,Issuing Bus passes

Periodically checking the documents of the buses (College & Contract)

Rules and Regulation

All the students who are willing to avail transport facility have to register with transport department. The Registration fee for 1st year and new joining students is Rs.500/-. Registration fee for existing students is Rs.250/-.

All the students who will register for availing transport facility have to pay half of the transport fee at the time of registration and remaining half of the fee within one month from the registration date

The transport fee is only for one academic year

There is no registration facility for each semester.
