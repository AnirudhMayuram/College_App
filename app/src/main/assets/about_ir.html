About International Relations (IR)

Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering & Technology has extended its educational frontiers worldwide by introducing its reputed courses to the foreign students in search of the right direction. From the year 2009 this initiative was started and is going on successfully till date.

The Directorate of University Foreign Relations is the body constituted to take care of each and every matter concerning their welfare. Students from various countries join here and pass out in flying colors and are excelling in every field both nationally and internationally thus giving us global fame.

There has been a lot of demand / enthusiasm from foreign students for their admission into various courses being offered by SVCET. From year 2009 the University has initiated its move to welcome the foreign students into JNTU.
