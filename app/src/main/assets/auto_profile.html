AUTOMOBILE DEPT PROFILE

The Department of Automobile Engineering has come into existence in the year 2014 with an intake of 60.

The department has 10 eminent staff members teaching 7, non teaching 2, supporting staff 1. One staff member with Ph.D, Two pursuing Ph.D and all have P.G. qualification.

The syllabus have been framed to meet the current Automobile Industry requirement.The department is equipped with fully established labs and has state of art technology.

Dr.C.Vijaya Bhaskar Reddy,Head of the Department has published more than 15 papers at various National and International conferences and various peer reviewed journals and applied for patent on ”Polymer Piezoresistive micro cantilever CO sensor and Method of making the same ( IPN No: 2276/MUM/2012)“.
